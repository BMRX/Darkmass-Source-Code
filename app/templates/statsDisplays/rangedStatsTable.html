Note: When inspecting a weapon in-game, the bars are seperated into two rows. There are 2 across the top row, and 3 along the second row.

The columns in this table simply represents this. Bars 1-2 are equivalent to the top row, and bars 3-5 the bottom row. There is currently a total of 12 different bars for melee weapons.

Damage is always present on every weapon. You may notice that some bars such as Moblity appear in different columns (2 and 5 in this case).

These do not differ in any way and share the same modifers as above. This table is simply just a representation of how they are displayed in-game.
