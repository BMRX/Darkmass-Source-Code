The ranged data is a lot more complicated than melee weapons. For Example, for melee weapons, A Krourk MK IV Cleaver And Rashad MK II Combat Axe Both Have A Penetration Bar, Which Effects Light Attack Damage Vs Flak Armoured, Etc In The Exact Same Fashion.

This does not follow the same pattern for ranged weapons. Whilst the Boltgun and Combat Shotgun share the Stopping Power bar, the modifiers inside that bar are completely different.

The best way to display this data in a readable fashion is through showing the bars per weapon, alongside the SPECIFIC modifiers tied to the bars of that particular weapon. This data can be viewed by clicking the weapon table button and then filtering by weapon through the dropdowns.

We are working on gathering data regarding the exact number ranges per modifier, per weapon. This will be implemented across the site once completed. Expect to also see the rate of fire, reload speed, and amount of bullet per burst (where applicable) in the near future.
