All classes have a base crit rate of 5%, aside from the Veteran who has 10%. The rates from the weapons in this table are additive, or in a few cases subtractive. For example, a Veteran with a Combat Blade would have 25% crit, whereas an Ogryn with a Heavy Stubber Would have 3%. The weapons with variable ranges such as the Tactical Axe all have the Critical Bonus bar, which has a modifier that directly defines their bonus crit chance.
